Social Phobia Analysis

Decoding Social Phobia Dynamics: Insights from SPIN Levels in the 18-28 Age Group. This study closely analyzes social phobia dynamics, focusing on SPIN levels in the 18-28 age group. By considering age-specific factors, we aim to reveal patterns and predictors, improving our precision in addressing social phobia in this age range

Top Accuracy-Based Model

Introducing our top 3 machine learning models with an outstanding 99% accuracy on categorical social phobia test data. Random Forest leads at 99.97%, showcasing robustness. Close behind is k-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) at 99.27%, known for simplicity. Logistic Regression is third at 99.12%, providing efficiency and interpretability. This impressive accuracy underlines their suitability for social phobia assessments.
